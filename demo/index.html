Pixel Union Labs

At Pixel Union we are constantly pushing the limits of Tumblr. We make awesome themes and sometimes we need to create things from scratch to make sure our users get the best possible experience.

Occasionally these experiments lead us into unknown territory. Some of them get scrapped, and others make their way into our themes. Yadda yadda.

Tumblr's Photoset Enhanced to Meet Today's User Standards

We knew that users loved selecting a layout for their photoset built right into the Tumblr backend but we found it to be limiting in both functionality and, well, fun.

So we put on our thinking caps and set out to recreate and improve one of Tumblr's coolest features. Enter Pixel Union Photoset Extended:

  • Captions and EXIF data tooltip
  • Enable high resolution images for photosets greater than 500px wide
  • Customizable options like rounded corners, bigger gutters, and more
